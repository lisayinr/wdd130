Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to attract customers to choose Fire Technologies Int. Inc. to complete their fire protection services. This website would serve to provide potential customers with information about the company and the different services they provide.

Audience

The audience for this website would be building owners, business owners, and particularly restaurant owners because they all require fire extinguishers in their buildings and other fire protection services.
